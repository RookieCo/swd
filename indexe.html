Origin of SWD

SWD Urethane Co. was found at the end of 60th century which followed the trend of hi-tech industry for the development of Southwest. Our name is derived from "Southwest Development"


Our headquarter is located in Mesa, Arizona USA

SWD has grown to supply polyurethane and polyurea products for a number of industries across the United States and Canada. SWD(Shanghai) is the affiliated manufacturing operation in SWD


We have tight cooperation with the Arizona University. Our R&D team has been developed many modern coating products, that is one of the reasons why we continue occupy the leading position compare other companies.

At the begining of 21st century, SWD started the investment in China. Fist we established the HUA SWD Polyurethane at Hubei Province, then SWD New Material(Shanghai) was invested as a professional coating company for PU corrosion protection, waterproof and insulation industry.


Come along with the business expansion, SWD have made another investment to establish SWD Hi-Tech Material(Jiangsu) Co., Ltd. Which is loacaed at the Nantong Binhai Industrial Park. It covers an area of 16,000 square meters
