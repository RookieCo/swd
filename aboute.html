SWD JIANGSU

SWD High-tech Material (Jiangsu) Co., as a high tech production base which is registered capital of RMB20,000,000 and has invested RMB60,000,000 for construction and building. Situated in Jiangsu Nantong national coastal industrial park, it is a comprehensive enterprise gathering scientific research, production, sales, technical after sale services which offers five leading series coating product series as spray polyurea, spray polyurethane anticorrosion, waterproof, reinforcement, flooring and insulation.


Based on the strong technical supports from SWD USA, SWD Shanghai has developed two component spray polyurea elastomer coating series, single component hand applied thick film polyurea elastomer coating series, single component moisture cured rigid polyurethane heavy-duty corrosion protection coating series, special performance corrosion protection coating series, spray polyurethane rigid foam series, casting polyurethane rigid foam artificial wood materials etc.

Along with the completion of SWD High-tech Material (Jiangsu) Co., Ltd, the annual production capacity of SWD Shanghai has a predominant position in the polyurea coating industry. To be specific, two component polyurea is 5,000 tons, two component polyruethane anticorrosion coating is 2,000 tons, single component hand applied polyurea industrial anticorrosion coating is 2,000 tons, single component polyurea building waterproof coating is 2,000 tons, single component thick film polyurea anticorrosion waterpoof wearable coating for engineering dam reinforcement repairment and impermeable reinforcement is 1,000 tons, polyaspartic anticorrosion waterproof top coating is 2,000 tons, single component moisture cured polyurethane heavy anticorrosion coating is 2,000 tons, additionally the production capacity for single component high temperature anticorrosion coating, special performance anticorrosion coating, one component water based industrial anticorrosion coating etc.


In order to adapt to the requirements of global low carbon economy, SWD New Material (Shanghai) Co., Ltd commits to develop, produce and sell eco-friendly products. The spray polyurea coating series has been appraised to be an excellent brand by International Solid Virescence Promotion Organization. The spray polyurethane foam for use as roof and wall insulation which has been listed as one of the recommended products by National Advanced Energy Effective Construction Materials.
